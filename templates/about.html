About Breast Cancer Prediction System

Our advanced AI-powered system helps medical professionals detect and classify breast cancer with high accuracy, enabling early intervention and personalized treatment plans.

Early Detection

Our system uses advanced machine learning models to detect breast cancer in its early stages, helping to save lives through timely diagnosis.

Stage Classification

The system predicts not only malignancy but also stages of cancer progression, aiding doctors in treatment planning.

Patient Data Analysis

Structured patient data is analyzed to understand risk factors such as age, tumor size, menopause status, and medical history.

Image-Based Prediction

Deep learning CNN models are used to classify medical images for accurate identification of cancerous tissues.

High Accuracy

Our models are trained on extensive datasets to ensure reliable predictions with minimal false positives.

User-Friendly Interface

Designed with healthcare professionals in mind, our interface is intuitive and easy to navigate.
